Common PK/PD Equations

One-Compartment Models

IV Bolus

C(t) = C_0 \cdot e^{-k_e t} = \frac{D}{V} \cdot e^{-k_e t}

IV Infusion (during infusion)

C(t) = \frac{R}{CL} \cdot (1 - e^{-k_e t}) = \frac{R}{k_e \cdot V} \cdot (1 - e^{-k_e t})

Steady-State Concentration (IV Infusion)

C_{ss} = \frac{R}{CL} = \frac{R}{k_e \cdot V}

Oral Dosing (First-Order Absorption)

C(t) = \frac{F \cdot D \cdot k_a}{V \cdot (k_a - k_e)} \cdot (e^{-k_e t} - e^{-k_a t})

Time to Peak (Oral)

T_{max} = \frac{\ln(k_a / k_e)}{k_a - k_e}

Two-Compartment Model (IV Bolus)

C(t) = A \cdot e^{-\alpha t} + B \cdot e^{-\beta t}

where: \alpha + \beta = k_{10} + k_{12} + k_{21} \alpha \cdot \beta = k_{10} \cdot k_{21}

Noncompartmental Analysis

AUC (Linear Trapezoidal)

AUC_{0 \to t_{last}} = \sum_{i=1}^{n-1} \frac{(C_i + C_{i+1})}{2} \cdot (t_{i+1} - t_i)

AUC to Infinity

AUC_{0 \to \infty} = AUC_{0 \to t_{last}} + \frac{C_{last}}{k_e}

Clearance (IV)

CL = \frac{Dose}{AUC_{0 \to \infty}}

Volume of Distribution (IV)

V_d = \frac{Dose}{k_e \cdot AUC_{0 \to \infty}}

Mean Residence Time (IV)

MRT = \frac{AUMC}{AUC}

Half-Life

t_{1/2} = \frac{\ln(2)}{k_e}

Pharmacodynamic Models

Emax Model

E = E_0 + \frac{E_{max} \cdot C}{EC_{50} + C}

Sigmoid Emax Model

E = E_0 + \frac{E_{max} \cdot C^\gamma}{EC_{50}^\gamma + C^\gamma}

Linear Model

E = E_0 + S \cdot C

Log-Linear Model

E = m \cdot \ln(C) + b

PK/PD Link Models

Effect Compartment

\frac{dC_e}{dt} = k_{e0} \cdot (C_p - C_e)

Indirect Response (Type I — Inhibit k_{in})

\frac{dR}{dt} = k_{in} \cdot \left(1 - \frac{I_{max} \cdot C}{IC_{50} + C}\right) - k_{out} \cdot R

Nonlinear PK

Michaelis–Menten Elimination

\frac{dC}{dt} = -\frac{V_{max} \cdot C}{K_m + C}

Saturable Protein Binding

C_{bound} = \frac{B_{max} \cdot C_{free}}{K_D + C_{free}}

Multiple Dosing

Accumulation Factor

R_{acc} = \frac{1}{1 - e^{-k_e \tau}}

Average Steady-State Concentration (Oral)

\bar{C}_{ss} = \frac{F \cdot D}{CL \cdot \tau}

Cmax at Steady State (IV Bolus)

C_{ss,max} = \frac{D}{V} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - e^{-k_e \tau}}

Cmin at Steady State (IV Bolus)

C_{ss,min} = \frac{D}{V} \cdot \frac{e^{-k_e \tau}}{1 - e^{-k_e \tau}}

Bioavailability

Absolute Bioavailability

F = \frac{AUC_{extravascular} \cdot Dose_{IV}}{AUC_{IV} \cdot Dose_{extravascular}}

Hepatic Clearance (Well-Stirred Model)

CL_H = \frac{Q_H \cdot f_u \cdot CL_{int}}{Q_H + f_u \cdot CL_{int}}

Allometric Scaling

Y = a \cdot W^b

Typical exponents: - Clearance: b = 0.75 - Volume of distribution: b = 1.0 - Rate constants: b = -0.25

## One-Compartment Models

### IV Bolus
$$C(t) = C_0 \cdot e^{-k_e t} = \frac{D}{V} \cdot e^{-k_e t}$$

$$C_{ss} = \frac{R}{CL} = \frac{R}{k_e \cdot V}$$

$$C(t) = \frac{F \cdot D \cdot k_a}{V \cdot (k_a - k_e)} \cdot (e^{-k_e t} - e^{-k_a t})$$

$$T_{max} = \frac{\ln(k_a / k_e)}{k_a - k_e}$$

where:
$$\alpha + \beta = k_{10} + k_{12} + k_{21}$$
$$\alpha \cdot \beta = k_{10} \cdot k_{21}$$

## Noncompartmental Analysis

$$AUC_{0 \to t_{last}} = \sum_{i=1}^{n-1} \frac{(C_i + C_{i+1})}{2} \cdot (t_{i+1} - t_i)$$

### AUC to Infinity
$$AUC_{0 \to \infty} = AUC_{0 \to t_{last}} + \frac{C_{last}}{k_e}$$

### Clearance (IV)
$$CL = \frac{Dose}{AUC_{0 \to \infty}}$$

### Volume of Distribution (IV)
$$V_d = \frac{Dose}{k_e \cdot AUC_{0 \to \infty}}$$

### Mean Residence Time (IV)
$$MRT = \frac{AUMC}{AUC}$$

### Half-Life
$$t_{1/2} = \frac{\ln(2)}{k_e}$$

## PK/PD Link Models

### Effect Compartment
$$\frac{dC_e}{dt} = k_{e0} \cdot (C_p - C_e)$$

$$\frac{dR}{dt} = k_{in} \cdot \left(1 - \frac{I_{max} \cdot C}{IC_{50} + C}\right) - k_{out} \cdot R$$

$$C_{bound} = \frac{B_{max} \cdot C_{free}}{K_D + C_{free}}$$

### Accumulation Factor
$$R_{acc} = \frac{1}{1 - e^{-k_e \tau}}$$

$$\bar{C}_{ss} = \frac{F \cdot D}{CL \cdot \tau}$$

$$C_{ss,max} = \frac{D}{V} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - e^{-k_e \tau}}$$

## Bioavailability

### Absolute Bioavailability
$$F = \frac{AUC_{extravascular} \cdot Dose_{IV}}{AUC_{IV} \cdot Dose_{extravascular}}$$

### Hepatic Clearance (Well-Stirred Model)
$$CL_H = \frac{Q_H \cdot f_u \cdot CL_{int}}{Q_H + f_u \cdot CL_{int}}$$

---

## Allometric Scaling

$$Y = a \cdot W^b$$

Typical exponents:
- Clearance: $b = 0.75$
- Volume of distribution: $b = 1.0$
- Rate constants: $b = -0.25$
